# Winning Style — distilled from the example library

**What this is:** the user's real top-performing carousels, studied once and written down here so the
writers do NOT have to open 80 screenshots every run. The raw images live in `reference/examples/` as
an archive — **do not open them per run; read this instead.** Emulate the vibe; **never copy** a topic
or wording. **Updated manually:** when the user shares a new winner in chat, its ONE new lesson is
folded in here (and the file is kept short).

## The look — filming grammar (this drives your `direction` & `shot_list`)
- **Cover = a real selfie or a cozy prop shot, never a designed graphic or logo.** Reaction/showcase →
  an excited selfie to camera ("OMG die geleakten Karten…"). Advice → a cozy bedroom/desk with a plush
  or sealed product in frame (a Piplup plush in glasses, cards on a bed). One bold line on top.
- **Body slides are REAL PHOTOS, not stock art:** cards fanned in hand, cards spread on a bed/table, a
  zip-binder, a plush, people playing at a table. The card ART or the real prop IS the visual.
- **Text sits in rounded colored pills / boxes or translucent dark bars placed directly over the
  photo.** A title/hook pill (often purple); good/neutral points in one colour (green), warnings in
  another (red); a translucent dark bar over a busy photo for legibility.
- **One tasteful emoji accent per line** (💜 👀 🥲 ✨). Never stacked.
- **Feel: handmade, personal, slightly imperfect — the opposite of an ad.** No logo, no polished
  "designed" look.

## The four proven templates (match to your assigned format)
1. **Reaction / leak-favorites showcase** — selfie cover ("…hier sind meine Favoriten"), then ONE
   full-frame card per slide with a colored **superlative-label banner** ("die coolsten zwei" / "die
   gefährlichsten zwei"). Near-zero copy; card art is the content. May run long. Keep every card on-topic.
2. **Numbered advice** ("N trucs / N Dinge…", often with "Partie 2") — selfie/prop cover with the
   number + optional Part serialization, then numbered slides (1/2/3), a title pill + 1–3 dash-bullets
   each over a relevant photo. Tight (≈5–6). Warm, personal regret/empathy — never veteran-flex.
3. **Anti-FOMO / emotional real-talk** — cozy prop cover, each slide ONE concrete beat over a card
   photo (a card that lost value; "willst du sie noch bei 0€?"; "sammel was dir gefällt"); close on a
   warm reframe. Concrete beats, never platitudes.
4. **Single-card / favorite spotlight** — one hero card, a personal or lore reason, a soft twist.
